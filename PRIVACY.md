# Privacy Policy — Ask ChatGPT Multi

**Last updated:** 2026-04-16

## Summary

This extension collects nothing, transmits nothing, and stores nothing outside your browser.

## What the extension does

- Runs only on `chatgpt.com` and `chat.openai.com`
- When you click the native **"Ask ChatGPT"** popup or the extension's **📎 Quote** fallback button, it reads the text you have selected on the page and writes it into the ChatGPT composer as a markdown blockquote
- That's the entire operation

## What the extension does not do

- Does not send any data to any server operated by the extension author
- Does not include any analytics, telemetry, or tracking SDKs
- Does not read or modify any page other than ChatGPT
- Does not access your clipboard, cookies, history, passwords, downloads, or any browser storage
- Does not use `chrome.storage`, `chrome.cookies`, or background network requests

## What data goes to chatgpt.com

The same data that would go there if you typed manually: whatever you put in the composer and click Send. The extension is a text-entry helper; it has no influence on what chatgpt.com does with your messages. Refer to [OpenAI's privacy policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy) for how they handle ChatGPT conversations.

## Source

The extension is open source ([GitHub](https://github.com/pranay0064/chatgpt-ask-multi)) and under 150 lines of runtime code. You can audit everything it does.
